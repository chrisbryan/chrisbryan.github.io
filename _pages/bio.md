Chris Bryan is an [assistant professor](https://isearch.asu.edu/node/311265/) of Computer Science at [Arizona State University](https://www.asu.edu/) in the [School of Computing, Informatics, & Decision Systems](https://cidse.engineering.asu.edu/) (CIDSE). He received his Ph.D. in 2018 from the University of California, Davis, as a part of the [VIDI lab](https://vidi.cs.ucdavis.edu). His research spans the areas of data visualization, human-computer interaction, and data science. At ASU, he heads the Sonoran Visualization Laboratory ([SVL @ ASU](https://svl-at-asu.github.io/)).
